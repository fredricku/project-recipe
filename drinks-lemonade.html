Drinks

Ingredients

  • 4 lemons, juiced
  • 1 quart water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 14 frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar

Lemonade

Insructions

In a 2 quart pitcher, combine the lemon juice, water and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Chill in refrigerator.
